About Us


Coffee Break is pleased to be the first coffee shop in South Boston to serve "Stumptown Coffee" and "Indian Coffee" products exclusively. Stumptown, originating in Portland Oregon had been named "Best Coffee in the World" by NPR, The New York Times, Food & Wine Magazine, and USA Today. To ensure exceptional quality and freshness we obtain our beans directly from Stumptown's roasting facility in Brooklyn, New York and Karnataka,India.


Opened its doors in December 2010, Coffee Break is ideally located just steps from the Boston Common. We are committed to providing the premier coffee experience in Boston by serving only the finest coffees, teas, and freshly baked pastries. COFFEE BREAK PHILOSOPHY Using only the finest coffee beans from various regions around the globe Offering outstanding service from knowledgeable baristas willing to educate our guests about coffee Giving back to the local community through involvement in various charity organizations Providing a cozy, enjoyable ambience for all to enjoy Creating excitement for high-end specialty coffee in the Greater Boston area
